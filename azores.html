The Azores

Atlantic Volcanic Archipelago - Geothermal Wonders & Marine Sanctuaries

Europe's Sustainable Island Paradise

The Azores archipelago leads in sustainable tourism with UNESCO biosphere reserves, geothermal energy powering most homes, and a commitment to preserving its unique volcanic landscapes and marine ecosystems.

Geothermal Energy & Innovation

Over 50% of the islands' energy comes from geothermal sources. Visitors can see this sustainable energy in action while supporting eco-friendly accommodations that utilize these natural resources.

Marine Conservation

The Azores are a sanctuary for whales, dolphins, and marine life. Our whale-watching tours follow strict guidelines and contribute to marine research and conservation programs.
