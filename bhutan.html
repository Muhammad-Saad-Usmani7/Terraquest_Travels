Bhutan

The Last Shangri-La - Where Gross National Happiness Measures Success

The Kingdom of Happiness

Bhutan is the world's only carbon-negative country, making it a pioneer in sustainable tourism. The nation measures its success not by GDP, but by Gross National Happiness, prioritizing the well-being of its people and environment.

Sustainable Development Fee

All visitors contribute to Bhutan's sustainable development through a daily fee that funds education, healthcare, and environmental protection projects. This ensures tourism directly benefits local communities and conservation efforts.

Cultural Preservation

Bhutan maintains strict building codes to preserve traditional architecture and limits tourist numbers at sacred sites to protect cultural heritage. Visitors experience authentic traditions that have been maintained for centuries.
